Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Empowering technology, entrepreneurship, education, and research to drive Bangladesh's growth and global competitiveness forward.

Sustainability

Promoting green energy, conservation, education, sustainable agriculture, and resilience to ensure Bangladesh's future prosperity.

Infrastructure

Developing transport, energy, digital networks, and proper planning to support Bangladesh's growth and connectivity.

Empowerment

Education, equality, entrepreneurship, and community development to uplift and empower every citizen of Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

As Chief Adviser, I envision a Bangladesh driven by inclusive development. Our priorities lie in education, healthcare, infrastructure, and technological advancement. Together, we will foster innovation, empower communities, and ensure sustainable growth. Through unity and dedication, we will build a resilient nation, ready to face global challenges and embrace opportunities.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
